## S&G 2026 goals

**All:** actively support ICT's long-term strategic improvement goals.

- **For working group members:** contribute to at least 3 tangible kaizen activities (at least 1 as lead) by year-end.
- **For others:** support the ICT strategy indirectly via your BAU role, as appropriate.

**Strategy Team:**

1. Define and communicate to ICT and business stakeholders the end-to-end processes, from identification of business need through value generation, by end of Q3
2. Identify and realize kaizen opportunities to improve information clarity and support sound business decision making:
   - a. Agree within ICT on the portfolio items in scope for 2026 global portfolio tracking and re-start a portfolio review process that can effectively monitor and influence progress on this portfolio by April-end
   - b. Evaluate portfolio tracking tool options, executing short term improvements to the existing tools and selecting a long-term go-to platform by year-end
   - c. Working closely with Finance, ODX and other stakeholders, re-define and optimize the processes around annual investment planning and DIB and include in 2027 planning guidance, including but not limited to: intake details and method, criteria for categorization, confirmation of business value, prioritization, human resource and budget constraints, ongoing management processes
   - d. Consider options to visualize and communicate ICT's lifecycle health from multiple angles (business applications, hardware, OS, software: version, state of supportability, known vulnerabilities, etc.) and
3. Facilitate the definition of 2027 ICT priorities according to the planning deadlines, including identification of long-term opportunities and gathering of roadmap input from ICT sub-functions and Business Partners
4. Support the Service Management team to transition our business application inventory to ServiceNow, including metadata definitions, data clean-up, maintenance and utilization of this data by year-end

**Governance Team:**

1. Establish the basics of a functional Vendor Management Office:
   - a. Populate, announce and actively maintain ICT's vendor master and contract master by June-end
   - b. Define the classification of and identify strategic vendors and major outsourcing engagements by September-end
   - c. Support completion of at least 1 cost reduction effort over 10m JPY by year-end, *stretch: support achieving over 50m in optimization*
   - d. Develop an initial vendor strategy plan by year-end with clear targets for sourcing opportunities and further process kaizen to be executed in 2027
   - e. Document and communicate process flows to facilitate effective vendor management by year-end, including but not limited to: contracting, performance management, risk management, strategic sourcing engagements
2. Develop an ICT talent framework:
   - a. Define a framework of talent needs across ICT by August-end
   - b. Conduct a survey to identify ICT's current state of skills and experience relative to the target framework and perform a gap analysis to identify opportunities by October-end
   - c. Work with HR and other stakeholders to promote development content to up-skill our existing staff and re-start a targeted program by year-end
3. Lead ongoing centralized payment handling and overall ICT forecast management to land within 5% of ICT's plan for FY 2026
4. Investigate opportunities to optimize S&G administrative processes and implement at least 3 improvements by year-end
5. Support ICT's Finance Business Partner to develop a clear and comprehensive 2027 annual plan *according to the planning deadlines*
6. Work with various stakeholders to effectively and efficiently address record management process kaizen, as needed

**Finance Partner:**

1. Lead the monthly budget tracking processes, including analysis of gaps and opportunities and smooth information exchange with ICT members
2. Lead the annual planning process, including timing explanation of timing and processes to smoothly submit a 2027 plan in line with financial guidance
3. Support ICT's cost optimization ambition with insightful data and analysis to support effective decision making

## 3人のチームマネジャーがまずすべきこと

### 1. 目標の「自分ごと化」と優先順位の整理

このドキュメントはリーダーが設定した**チームレベルの目標**です。マネジャーはこれをそのまま受け取るのではなく、**自チームのメンバーに落とし込んだ個人目標（Individual Goals）に翻訳する**必要があります。

具体的には、各目標に対して：
- 誰が担当するか（オーナーの割り当て）
- いつまでにどの状態にするか（マイルストーンの設定）
- 何をもって「達成」とするか（成功基準の明確化）

を自分たちで定義し、リーダーに提示・合意を取ることが期待されています。

### 2. 期限の把握と逆算スケジュールの作成

文書には「April-end」「June-end」「August-end」「October-end」「by year-end」と複数の締め切りが明示されています。マネジャーは**最も早い締め切り（4月末）から逆算して、今すぐ動くべきことを特定**する必要があります。

特に早急に動くべきもの：
- **Strategy Team** → ポートフォリオレビューの再始動（4月末）
- **Governance Team** → ベンダーマスター・契約マスターの整備（6月末）
- **Finance Partner** → 月次予算管理プロセスの即時始動（定常業務）

### 3. 他チーム・他部門との連携の段取り

目標全体を通じて「Finance・ODX・HR・Business Partners・Service Management」など**他部門との連携が必須**の項目が非常に多いです。マネジャーは早い段階で関係者に連絡を取り、協力関係を構築しておく必要があります。これを後回しにすると、締め切り直前に動けなくなるリスクがあります。

## リーダーが期待しているもの

### アクション面
- **自律的な計画立案**：「何をすればいいですか」と聞くのではなく、自分で考えた計画をリーダーに提示する姿勢
- **定期的な進捗報告**：締め切りが近づいてから報告するのではなく、早期に課題を共有する
- **Stretch Goalへの意識**：Governance Teamのコスト削減で「50m最適化」という上乗せ目標が明示されており、最低ラインをクリアするだけでなく、**より高い水準を目指す姿勢**が求められている

### フィードバック面
リーダーが期待するフィードバックのタイミングと内容：

| タイミング | 期待されるフィードバック |
|---|---|
| **目標受領直後（今）** | 目標の理解確認、不明点の質問、懸念事項の共有 |
| **計画策定後（近日中）** | 担当割り当てと実行計画の提示・合意 |
| **各マイルストール前後** | 進捗状況の報告、必要に応じてリソースや支援の要請 |
| **年度末** | 達成度の振り返りと次年度への示唆 |

## 3チームに共通する重要な視点

**「at least 3 tangible kaizen activities」という全員共通の目標**が冒頭に置かれていることは重要です。リーダーは単に与えられた仕事をこなすことではなく、**自分たちで課題を見つけ、改善を主導する姿勢**をすべてのチームに求めています。

これはマネジャーが部下に対しても同じことを求めるべきであり、チーム全体で「改善の文化」を実践することがリーダーの根本的な期待と言えます。
